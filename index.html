My Languange

Cyber Attack

Cyber attacks are attacks carried out online with the aim of damaging computer systems, stealing data, or disrupting digital services. These attacks can include hacking, malware distribution, or malware attacks such as ransomware and denial-of-service (DoS). Cyber attacks can affect both individuals and organizations and can have motives such as criminal, political, or personal. Cyber attackers use various means to carry out attacks, such as using malicious programs, circumvention, or distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attacks. Cyber attacks can cause financial losses, reputational damage, and loss of customer trust. Companies should be aware of and organize digital security to prevent cyber attacks and reduce the risk of loss.

Network Analysis

Network analysis is the process of mapping and analyzing interactions between people or systems in a network. This method uses graph theory and topology to analyze networks consisting of vertices (nodes) and edges (connectors). Network analysis can be used to determine interaction relationships, friendships, and network structure. This process can provide information such as interaction and friendship relationships between users, trends and patterns, and the influence of influencers. This method can be used in social network development, data analysis, and social behavior measurement. Network analysis has many benefits, such as discovering trends and patterns, testing hypotheses, finding influencers, and conducting sentiment analysis.

Peneration Testing

Penetration testing or pentest is the process of testing the security of a computer system or network by officially simulating a cyber attack. The purpose of pentest is to find vulnerabilities in the system and prevent possible attacks from irresponsible parties.

Computer Network

Computer network is a system that connects computers between computers or with other devices through communication media. In a computer network, computers can communicate with each other, using certain equipment such as cables or wirelessly (wireless).
